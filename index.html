The Ultimate Battle of Heroes vs Villians

1) Choose your Hero to enter the Battle Zone. Then choose the Villian to fight against. All characters have different stats so choose your allies and opponents wisely!

2) When you've chosen your characters, hit the Start Battle button to begin the mayhem

3) Once the battle has begun, click the attack button to do damage to the other character. Remember they will also return with an attack

4) Heroes and Villians may be swapped in and out of the battle zone at any time

5) Damage is calculated based on the characters power levels and opponents defense levels

6) The game is won once the final boss is defeated. If all your Heroes parish, you lose.

TIP: Try using high defense characters against low attack characters
